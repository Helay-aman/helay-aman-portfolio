My Story

Hello! I'm Helay Aman, and I'm a cpmputer programming student. My journey into the world of web development began with a curious click and has since evolved into a passion for crafting engaging digital experiences.

I believe that code is a powerful medium for storytelling and problem-solving. My approach combines a keen eye for design with a logical mind for functionality. I love learning new technologies and constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible with HTML and CSS.

Outside of coding, you can find me doing gym, exploring nature, reading novels, brewing coffee. These activities fuel my creativity and provide a fresh perspective that I bring to my projects. I'm excited to share my work and connect with fellow creators!
